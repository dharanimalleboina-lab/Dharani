Lol Sabha

About the Event

Get ready for laughter, wit, and unstoppable punchlines! Lol Sabha is the stage where humor rules and confidence shines.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Open to all students. Solo performances only.
  • Time limit: 5–7 minutes. Exceeding the time may lead to disqualification.
  • Content must be original. Plagiarism is strictly not allowed.
  • No vulgar, abusive, or offensive content (religion, caste, gender, or personal attacks are prohibited).
  • Participants must report 30 minutes before the event begins.

Judging criteria

  • Originality and creativity of the performance.
  • Clarity of expression and communication skills.
  • Adherence to the theme of the event.
  • Overall impact and engagement of the presentation.
