Digital Dhamka

About the Event

Where creativity meets strategy in the digital world. Digital Dhamaka challenges you to think big, market smart, and create impact.

Rules & Guidelines

  • The ad topic will be revealed on the spot.
  • Participants have 10-15 minutes to brainstorm and create their content.
  • They may use a projector, but only with what’s made in that prep time,no pre-prepared slides.
  • Participant gets 2 minutes to present their ad.
  • Any form of cheating or misbehavior will lead to disqualification.

Judging criteria

  • Originality and creativity of the ad concept.
  • Clarity of expression and communication skills.
  • Adherence to the given topic.
  • Overall impact and engagement of the presentation.
