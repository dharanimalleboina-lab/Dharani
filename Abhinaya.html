Abhinaya

About the Event

Step into a character and bring stories to life. Abhinaya is all about expression, confidence, and the magic of performance.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Maximum 2 participants per team.
  • Each performance must be under 5 minutes.
  • No foul, abusive, or offensive language is permitted. Content should be respectful and suitable for all audiences,
  • Participants must report 15 minutes before the event.
  • Participants are allowed to use simple props, costumes, or background scenery to enhance the performance.
  • Any setup must be completed within the allotted time.
  • Participants are responsible for bringing their own materials.

Judging criteria

  • Originality of the artwork
  • Clarity of the concept and execution
  • Acting skills & expression
  • Time management and adherence to guidelines
  • Dialogue delivery and clarity
  • Overall Composition
